INSTRUCTIONS:

1. Press start when ready to play and enter two player names
2. The computer will determine who gets the first go
3. Receive 7 random letters
4. Out of these letters, create a word from the English Dictionary
5. Place these letters on the board to score the most points
6. The first word placed on the board, must have a letter on the grey circle
7. A player cannot use another players letter that has been put down unless they have that letter as well
8. The more difficult the letter is to use,the higher the amount of points it is worth
9. The amount of letters you use will be replaced with letters from the pool
10. If you can't go, enter '0',this will move onto the next players turn and you will get 1 extra letter
11. The winner is the player with the most points when 7 rounds are finished!
